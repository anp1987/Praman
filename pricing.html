FAQ

Pricing questions

Is there really a free plan?

Yes. The Starter plan is genuinely free — no credit card required. You get a full assessment for one framework, a compliance score, and a gap report. It's designed to show you the value before you commit.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What counts as a "framework"?

Each compliance standard (DPDP, GDPR, ISO 27001, SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI DSS, etc.) counts as one framework. The Professional plan supports up to 5, and Enterprise is unlimited.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes — annual billing gives you 2 months free (pay for 10, get 12). Contact our sales team for annual pricing on Professional and Enterprise plans.

What's included in "Audit preparation support"?

Our Enterprise plan includes dedicated support during audit preparation — evidence review, control testing verification, gap remediation guidance, and live support during auditor interviews.

Do you offer discounts for startups?

Yes. We offer 50% off Professional plans for Y Combinator, Techstars, and other accelerator-backed startups in their first two years. Contact us with your accelerator details.
