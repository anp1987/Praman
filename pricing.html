Questions

Pricing FAQ

Do I have to start with the Assessment?

In most cases, yes — it's how we understand your data and scope the work accurately. If you've already done a recent assessment, we can review it and move straight to implementation.

What determines where I land in the price range?

Mainly the size of your business, how many systems hold personal data, and how complex your data flows are. We confirm the exact figure after a short scoping call.

Is the Retainer required?

No. It's optional, but most clients choose it because DPDP compliance needs upkeep as your product and the rules change.

Do you provide legal advice?

We provide compliance advisory and work alongside qualified privacy lawyers. Formal legal opinions come from your counsel, whom we're happy to collaborate with.
